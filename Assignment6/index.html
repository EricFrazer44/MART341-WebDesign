Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 medium length bananas (very ripe)
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cups of sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 all-purpose flower

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. In a medium bowl, mash bananas and add butter
  3. In the same bowl, mix in the remaining ingredients
  4. Pour contents into a loaf pan
  5. Bake in the oven for 45 to 55 minutes (or until a toothpick comes out clean from the center)
  6. Remove from the oven and let it cool in the pan for a few minutes
  7. Remove the bread from the pan and let cool
  8. Slice and serve!
