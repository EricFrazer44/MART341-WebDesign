My Top Video Games



Escape From Tarkov

This game is being developed by Battlestate Games and is growing in players. It is a game that focuses on realism. The objective is to go into a game and kill enemies and loot various goods found around the maps. After this there is only one goal, SURVIVE!


Fallout 4

Fallout 4 takes place in an apocolyptic wasteland where the world was ruined by a global atomic war. In this alternate reality the integration of nuclear power made all the technology different than our own, but at what cost. You emerge from a pre-war vault in search of your lost son, and find that the world you once knew has mutated into something totally different.


Destiny

Destiny is a game that takes place in the future where humanity has been pushed to the brink of extinction. You are a guardian, a warrior of the light, and you must fight to protect the last city on earth. You must fight to protect the traveler, a mysterious being that has given you the power to fight back against the darkness.
