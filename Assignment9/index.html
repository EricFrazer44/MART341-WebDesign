How to change the oil in your car

Step by step instructions

Step 1: Gather the necessary tools

First, you will need to gather the necessary tools to change the oil in your car. You will need:

  • Oil
  • Oil filter
  • Oil filter wrench
  • Oil pan
  • Funnel
  • Socket wrench
  • Jack
  • Jack stands
  • Shop rags
