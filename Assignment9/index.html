How to change the oil in your car

Step by step instructions

Step 1: Identify proper oil type and filter

You will need to first aquire the proper type of oil

  • Check your owner's manual
  • Check the oil cap
  • Check the oil filter

After aquiring the correct oil type, you need to ensure you have enough to properly put into the car

Step 2: Gather the necessary tools

Second, you will need to gather the necessary tools to change the oil in your car. You will need:

  • Oil
  • Oil filter
  • Oil filter wrench
  • Oil pan
  • Funnel
  • Socket wrench
  • Jack
  • Jack stands
  • Shop rags

Step 3: Prepare the car

Third, you will need to prepare the car for the oil change

  • Warm up the engine
  • Jack up the car
  • Place jack stands
  • Place oil pan

Step 4: Drain the oil

Fourth, you will need to drain the oil from the car

  • Locate the oil drain plug
  • Drain the oil
  • Replace the oil drain plug

Step 5: Replace the oil filter

Fifth, you will need to replace the oil filter

  • Locate the oil filter
  • Remove the oil filter
  • Rub a small amount of oil along the seam of the new filter
  • Replace the oil filter

Step 6: Add the new oil

Sixth, you will need to add the new oil to the car

  • Locate the oil cap
  • Place a funnel in the oil cap
  • Add the new oil
  • Replace the oil cap

Step 7: Check the oil level

Seventh, you will need to check the oil level

  • Run vehicle for a couple minutes
  • Turn off vehicle
  • Remove the dipstick
  • Wipe the dipstick clean
  • Replace the dipstick
  • Remove the dipstick
  • Check the oil level
  • If the oil is at a good level, all is good
  • If the oil is too low, add more as needed

Step 8: Clean up

Eighth, you will need to clean up

  • Remove the oil pan
  • Remove the jack stands
  • Lower the car
  • Remove the jack
  • Dispose of the old oil
  • Dispose of the old oil filter

Step 9: Record the oil change

Ninth, you will need to record the oil change

  • Record the date
  • Record the mileage
