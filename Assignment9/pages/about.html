Why did I chose to write this tutorial?

I was recently presented with the task of changing my oil, but it was not my first time doing this. I then thought about if I did not have any experience and how difficult this could be. This got me thinking about a way to present the process to someone who might have little experience.

What is the purpose of this tutorial?

The purpose of this tutorial is to provide a step by step guide to changing your oil. This tutorial will provide a list of tools needed, a list of materials needed, and a step by step guide to changing your oil.
