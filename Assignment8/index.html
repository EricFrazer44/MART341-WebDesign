Where is Roswell?

What Happened?

On July 8th, 1947, a rancher named Mac Brazel discovered some strange debris in his sheep pasture. The debris was described as "foil-like" and "metallic". He brought the debris to the local sheriff, who then brought it to the Roswell Army Air Field. The Air Force then released a statement saying that the debris was from a crashed weather balloon. However, many people believe that the debris was actually from a crashed UFO.

The evidence is out there!
